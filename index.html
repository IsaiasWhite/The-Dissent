"This is for all.
We must focus on gaining more insight to who we are as individuals."

In a collection of poems, anecdotes, and reflective essays based on real experiences and inspired by his faith in Jesus Christ, Isaiah White shares valuable truths while inviting others to focus on what is important, reflect on the world around them, and see life in a different light. While exploring his own journey, White traverses through the waters of “what it seems” to provide inspiration to others seeking their own truths and a new path forward.

About Isaiah

Isaiah is a Texas native that believes a person's upbringing does not have to conflict with the differences of others, but can complement those differences to push society closer to the truth of life.

“There’s a portion of us that exists that cannot be seen directly, but it can be spoken to.”
